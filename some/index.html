INTRODUCTION OF COUNTRY VILLA

Country Villa is an exquisite property that offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Unlike large metropolitan hotels, it specializes in providing a personalized and intimate experience, blending traditional charm with modern comforts. The hotel features a fine-dining restaurant specializing in local cuisine, a cozy pub, a well-maintained garden, and a refreshing swimming pool. The evolution from manual systems to modern hospitality practices is evident in their service, which focuses on warm, individual attention rather than just luxurious facilities. The true luxury of Country Villa lies in its peaceful ambiance and the genuine hospitality that makes every guest feel uniquely valued.

[Photo: Reception Area]

Front Office Department

The Front Office at Country Villa acts as the hotel's "Show Case Window," creating the first and last impression for all guests. As the "Nerve Center," it efficiently coordinates all guest services and inter-departmental communication. The department is responsible for welcoming guests, handling reservations, managing check-ins and check-outs, providing continuous information, and settling guest accounts. The staff performs their duties with great care, ensuring a smooth and memorable stay. Their functions also include luggage assistance and arranging local travel, making them key to the hotel's operational flow and guest satisfaction.

[Photo: A Clean Hotel Room]

Housekeeping Department

The Housekeeping department is the backbone of Country Villa's commitment to comfort and cleanliness. This team is dedicated to the meticulous upkeep of all rooms and public areas, ensuring a spotless, safe, and aesthetically pleasing environment for guests. Their responsibilities extend beyond cleaning to include laundering linen, restocking guest supplies, and ensuring the security of guest belongings. They also maintain the hotel's beautiful gardens and swimming pool, implement pest control, and coordinate closely with other departments to uphold the highest standards of hygiene and presentation throughout the property.

[Photo: Room Amenities]

Room

The rooms at Country Villa are designed to be a tranquil haven, combining rustic charm with modern convenience. Each room features large windows offering views of the serene gardens or the surrounding landscape. The facilities provided ensure a comfortable stay and include:

  • Toiletries
  • Mini Bar / Refrigerator
  • In-room Safes
  • High-speed Wireless Internet
  • Tea/Coffee Station
  • Hairdryer
  • Ironing Accessories (Upon Request)
  • Room Service
  • HVAC System
[Photo: Group Photo with Staff]

Conclusion

After visiting Country Villa, I found it to be a hidden gem offering peace and tranquility. The weather and atmosphere are distinctly calmer than the city, making it an ideal retreat. The food served is authentic and delicious, and the accommodation is comfortable and clean. The hotel successfully blends natural beauty with necessary modern amenities. The staff were incredibly friendly and approachable, always willing to help with any request. It is a beautiful property perfect for a weekend getaway with friends and family to relax and recharge.

Finally, I would like to thank Modern College Of Management for providing us with the wonderful opportunity to visit and learn from Country Villa.
